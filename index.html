Privacy Policy · Last updated May 4, 2026

SignTap is built with your privacy as the top priority. All autographs, signatures, and videos are stored exclusively on your device. We never collect, upload, or share your data.

What We Collect

SignTap uses your device's front-facing camera to record a short video during the autograph signing process. This video is stored locally on your device only, and is used solely to verify the authenticity and capture the moment of the autograph. Signatures are captured as digital images and stored locally on your device.

What We Don't Collect

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, location, or any other personal identifying information. We do not require an account to use SignTap. We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising.

Data Storage

All data collected through SignTap is stored exclusively on your device. We do not operate any servers or cloud storage. If you delete the app, all data is permanently and irreversibly removed from your device.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transmit any data to third parties. Ever. The Share Card feature shares only what you explicitly choose to share — an image of the autograph card — and only when you tap the Share button.

Consent to Recording

By using the signing feature in SignTap, all parties involved acknowledge and consent to being recorded by the device's front-facing camera for the purpose of autograph verification.

Age Requirement

SignTap is intended for users aged 18 and older. By using this app you confirm that you are at least 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under the age of 18.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control at all times. You can delete any autograph directly within the app, or uninstall the app to remove all data permanently.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated date at the top of this page.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the App Store support page for SignTap.
